Privacy Policy for LinkedBoost AI

Effective Date: July 7, 2026

LinkedBoost AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the extension collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Text and prompts entered by users to generate LinkedIn posts.
  • Extension settings and preferences stored locally.
  • Information required to communicate with AI services.

How We Use Information

  • Generate AI-powered LinkedIn posts.
  • Improve formatting and writing suggestions.
  • Store user preferences.
  • Maintain extension functionality.

Data Sharing

LinkedBoost AI does not sell or rent user data.

User prompts may be securely transmitted to AI providers solely to generate requested content.

Storage

Extension preferences are stored locally using Chrome Storage APIs.

Security

Reasonable safeguards are used to protect user information.
